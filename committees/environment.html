Environment and Sustainability

Described as “The Greatest Threat To Global Security” by the United Nations, climate change has been one of the foremost policy issues over the past few decades. The conservation of our planet’s natural resources and biodiversity, as well as combating the ever-increasing rate of global warming, are essential in the long-term wellbeing of the human race.

Our Environment and Sustainability committee explores issues related to the environment and the creation of sustainable policies.
